Tara Zedayko

Entrepreneurial Journey


Tara recognized that there was a gap in pet healthcare. She realized that when you take your dog to the vet, you usually get cookie-cutter treatment. As dog owners know, this can be frustrating due to the trial and error you must go through in order to solve your pets specific health issues. This is where the idea of individualized dog supplements spawned. Tara decided to begin the process of producing a nutritional supplement powder that can be formulated based on the specific needs of your pet. This would be the beginning of Dig Labs.
